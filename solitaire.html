Black Hole

Last Updated: September 6th, 2025

Black Hole is a variant of Golf. Invented by David Parlett, the objective of the game is to consolidate the whole deck into one pile, starting from the one card starting the wastepile.

As Block Hole is a game derivative of Golf, my Black Hole implementation naturally borrows heavily from my Golf implementation. There are some features I want to implement:

  • Fix the Ace of Spades as the initial wastepile card
  • In-browser play

Golf

Last Updated: August 18th, 2025


Golf is a builder game with the objective to clear a 7-by-5 "tableau" of cards and consolidate them into the wastepile. Available cards (the tableau's bottom-most cards) can only be added to the wastepile if they are one rank above or below the top wastepile card. The name comes from the ability to score "holes" by the number of cards left over. The score, accumulated over 9 holes, is then compared to a par of 36.

The first solitaire game that I implemented, I chose to use C++ as a language. There are some features that I want to add, including:

  • Scorekeeping
  • Ruleset Optiions
  • In-Browser Play
