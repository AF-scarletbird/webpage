001: Minesweeper

Locate some mines in the grid. To help you do so, some cells have been clued with numbers; these numbers indicate the amount of mines present in the immediate surroundings (the eight cells horizontally, vertically, or diagonally adjacent to the numbered cell).
Some cells are clued with a question mark (?). Like other clue cells, these cells do not contain. However, it is up to you to determine both where and how many mines are placed around these clues.
